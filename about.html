Hello! My name is Vivian. I'm currently a second year MSI student studying User Experience Design at the University of Michigan School, where I also earned a BA in Economics, a minor in Creative Writing, and won a Hopwood Award for poetry.

My interests lie in exploring the relationships between poetics, literature, and informational structures. I also hope to work towards the democratization of design, at the same time moving away from anthropocentrism and engaging with design justice.

If you'd like, you can also find me on Instagram and LinkedIn.
