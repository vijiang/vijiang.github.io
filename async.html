async: Text-Centric Social Media


A network focused on sharing the written word.
Created with React Native and Expo, in coordination with Stephanie Choi.


Target Audience

Our audience centers on writers who wish to share works of poetry, prose, fiction, essay, or free-form text-based thoughts.

The audience we have in mind knows about and uses other social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter to share their work and are comfortable using most mobile applications.

Value Proposition

There are currently little to no social media sites with a prmiary focus on on words and text, which makes sense as few people want to read long, unbroken blocks of words. However, for those who do, or for those who would like to read a friend’s short literary works without distractions, async could be their platform of choice.

We’d also like to keep this experience as organic and uninfluenced by algorithms as possible. Unlike traditional social media apps that harness social value “counters” such as friends,likes, and followers to determine levels of popularity, our app seeks instead to emphasize the viewing of the work itself.

The application will mostly be content-based, with the presence of user profiles allowing for users to maintain a collection of their posts as a cohesive unit of “work.”
