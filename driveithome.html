Rocket Mortgage: Drive It Home Sweepstakes


Desktop and mobile prototypes for sweepstakes landing pages and submission forms. Created as part of a Quicken Loans summer-long internship competition, as side project to our main duties.

Along with a team of professional designers, six fellow interns and I collaborated on the creation of marketing visuals (banners, email hero images, handouts, marketing copy, and the following prototypes for a summer internship project based on the creation of a national sweepstakes.

Our final products were collected and presented to a board of judges at the end of the summer period.

Sample Desktop Landing Page


Includes main marketing hero image at the top, followed by sweepstakes rules and information. Automotive theme emphasized with dashboard imagery for prize details.

Sample Mobile Submission Form


Clear and concise submission form for sweepstakes application. Includes brief questionnaires at the end to assess interest in company products, and to assist with lead allocation.
