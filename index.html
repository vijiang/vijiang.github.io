    My name is Vivian & I'm a second year master's student studying User Experience Design at the University of Michigan School of Information.

    Currently, I'm interested in exploring the relationships between poetics, literature, and informational structures, as well as the methods through which we can reclaim our attention from the pitfalls of persuasive design.
