I'm a second year master's student concentrating on User Experience Design at the University of Michigan's School of Information.

Through my work in this field, I seek to better understand means of representing, organizing, and presenting information through a framework of aesthetic experiences.

My other interests include poetry, fiction, film photography, Asian independent cinema, and playing around with music technology.
